MUTUAL NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

This Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement is made between Startup Founders LLC and Angel Investor Partners.

The parties wish to explore a potential business relationship and in connection with this may disclose confidential information to each other.

Each party agrees that any confidential information disclosed by the other party shall be used solely for evaluating the potential relationship and shall not be disclosed to any third party without prior written consent.

This confidentiality obligation shall remain in effect for two years from the date of disclosure, regardless of whether the parties enter into a further agreement.

This Agreement does not obligate either party to enter into any further business relationship or transaction.

Confidential information does not include information that is publicly available, independently developed, or rightfully received from a third party without restriction.

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of California.
